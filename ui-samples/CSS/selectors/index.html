    • Thasos

      Thasos is mountainous and densely wooded.The lower slopes of the mountain,
      covered by pine forests and olive groves, seem to tumble into to sea,
      all around the 100km long perimeter road of the island with the beautiful coastline,
      the numerous beaches, the captivating inlets and estuaries.

    • Lefkada

      The Ionian island Lefkada is the nearest one to the western side
      of the Greece mainland and it can even be reached by car, via a bridge!
      It is one of the most popular Ionian destinations, full of amazing beaches
      with turquoise waters, monasteries, museums, and seafront or mountainous
      villages with a unique charm.
